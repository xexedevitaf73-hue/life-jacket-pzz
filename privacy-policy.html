​Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We might get info in these ways:

a. Stuff You Tell Us Straight Up

*   **Personal Info:** Like your name, email, phone number, where to send stuff, and where you get billed.
*   **Account Info:** What you pick for a username, password, and what you like.
*   **Payment Info:** Your card info (don't worry, a safe third party handles this).
*   **What You Say:** Questions, what you think, stuff you send to customer service.
*   **Job Stuff:** If you're applying for a job, we might get your resume, where you worked before, and who can vouch for you.

b. Info We Get Automatically

*   What kind of device you're using, your IP address, operating system, browser, and language.
*   What you do on our site: which pages you peep, what links you hit, how long you chill, and when you swung by.
*   Where you are (if you let us know).
*   Cookies and similar tech (pixels, beacons, analytics stuff).

c. Info from Other People

*   Partners who help us sell or run our services.
*   Social media (if you log in that way).
*   Public records and other service companies.

**2. How We Put Your Info to Use**

We might use your info to:

*   Run our services and keep them going.
*   Help you sign up, make sure it's really you, and manage your account.
*   Answer your questions and lend a hand if you're having trouble.
*   See what's popular, make things better, and build new stuff.
*   Send you promos, newsletters, and updates (if you're cool with it).
*   Keep your account safe, stop weird activity, and guard our data.
*   Do what the law says, get our terms enforced, and sort out disputes.

**3. Who We Share Info With**

We don't sell your personal info. But we might share it with:

*   Service companies that host our site, analyze stuff, process payments, and do marketing.
*   Our sister companies (for boring, internal stuff).
*   If things change in business, like a merger or something.
*   If the law tells us to (court order, etc.).
*   If you say it's okay.

All these service companies have to keep your info secret and only use it for what we agreed on.

**4. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Know who you are and keep you logged in.
*   Remember what you like.
*   See what's getting used.
*   Show you ads (if that's something we're doing).

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but stuff might not work right.

**5. How Long We Keep Your Info**

We only keep your personal info as long as we need it for what we said in this policy, or as long as the law says we have to. When we don't need it anymore, we'll either delete it or make it anonymous.

**6. How We Keep Your Info Safe**

We use standard security stuff like encryption, secure data centers, and controls so people can't get to your data without permission, mess with it, or wreck it.

But no online security is perfect. You use our services knowing there's some risk.

**7. International Stuff**

If you're using our services from another country, your info might get sent to our servers in a different country. We'll make sure that transfer follows data protection rules.

**8. Your Rights**

Depending on where you live, you might be able to:

*   See your personal info and get a copy.
*   Fix wrong info.
*   Ask us to delete your info (be forgotten).
*   Tell us to stop using your info in certain ways.
*   Take back your permission.
*   Move your info to another service.

To do any of this, email us at [your email address]. We might need to check who you are first.

**9. California Rights**

If you live in California, you have extra rights, like asking us what info we have and how we use it, asking us to delete it, and saying no to selling it (which we don't do anyway).

**10. Europe and UK Rights**

If you're in Europe or the UK:

We're using your info because you said it was okay, it's part of our contract, the law says we need to, or we have a good reason.

If you're unhappy, you can talk to your local data protection people.

**11. Kids' Privacy**

We don't knowingly get info from kids under 13. If we find out we accidentally did, we'll delete it ASAP.

**12. Other Sites**

Our services might link to other sites. We're not in charge of what they do, so check out their privacy policies before you share anything.

**13. Policy Updates**

We might change this policy sometimes. We'll change the Last Updated date and post the new version on our website. If it's a big change, we might tell you directly.
