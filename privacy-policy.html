Privacy Policy

**1. What Info We Grab**

We might get these types of info:

a. Info You Give Us

*   **Account Stuff:** Name, email, phone number, password, where to send the bill.
*   **Payment Info:** Credit card numbers, billing details (kept safe by other companies).
*   **What You Say to Us:** Messages, questions, feedback, etc.
*   **Optional Stuff:** Things like your age group, survey answers, what you like.

b. Info We Get Automatically

*   **Device and Tech Details:** Your IP address, what kind of browser you use, what operating system, and special ID numbers for your device.
*   **How You Use Our Site:** Pages you check out, how long you stay, where you came from, what you click on.
*   **Cookies:** Small files that help the site work better and let us see how people are using it.

c. Info From Other Places

We might get info from:

*   Social media sites you use to log in (like Google, Facebook, or Apple).
*   Companies that process payments, keep track of website stats, and do marketing.
*   Public records or companies we work with.

**2. How We Use Your Info**

We might use your personal info to:

*   Keep the website running and available for service.
*   Process payments and finish transactions.
*   Take care of your account
*   Send you updates, special deals, and newsletters (if you sign up).
*   Make the website work better and make customers experience better.
*   Find and stop fraud or unauthorized access.
*   Follow legal requirements.
*   Do research and make reports.

We only use your info if we have a good reason, like if you agree, if it's part of a contract, or if it helps our business.

**3. Sharing Your Info**

We don't sell your personal info. We only share it if:

*   We use other companies to help us (like payment processors, IT support, website hosting, website stats, and email services).
*   It's with companies that are part of our company, for business reasons.
*   The law makes us share it with authorities.
*   Our company gets bought or merges with another company.
*   You say its okay to share.

All groups we worked with should keep your data confidential and protected.

**4. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Remember your settings.
*   Make the site work better.
*   See how the site is used.
*   Show you personalized ads (if we're allowed to).

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some parts of the site might not work right if you turn off cookies.

**5. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We keep your info only as long as we need it for the reasons listed in this policy, or as long as the law says we have to. When we don't need it anymore, we delete it or make it anonymous.

**6. How We Keep Your Data Safe**

*   We employed methods to protect your data
*   Encrypting private info.
*   Storing and transferring data safely.
*   Limiting who can access the data.
*   Checking our security regularly.

While we try our hardest, no internet data is 100% guaranteed.

**7. Your Choices**

Depending on where you live, you might have the right to:

*   See what personal data we have about you
*   Fix or change incorrect information.
*   Ask us to delete your personal data.
*   Tell us to stop using your data.
*   Take back your consent.
*   Get your data in a format you can take somewhere else.

To do any of these things, contact us at \[your email address]. We might need to check who you are before we do anything.

**8. Where Your Data Goes**

Your info might be sent to other countries. When required, we keep your data safe:

*   Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
*   Decisions by authorities.
*   Binding corporate rules

**9. Kids' Privacy**

We don't knowingly collect data from kids under 13. If we find out we have a child's data, we'll delete it quickly.

**10. Links to Other Sites**

Our website might have links to other sites. We're not in charge of their privacy practices. Check their privacy policies.

**11. Policy Updates**

We might change this policy sometimes. We'll post updates on this page with a new Last Updated date. We'll tell you about big changes.
